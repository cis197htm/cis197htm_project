Lasagna is Italian wide, flat pasta dish. It is made with tomato-meat or bechamel sauce layers between lasagna sheets, and of course a lot of cheese to make the lasagna more delicious. This flat, long, and wide pasta, possibly one of the oldest types of pasta. The lasagna can be made with chicken or beef. The first made recipe happened in the early 14th century in Naples, Italy.
